Welcome to Recipe Finder


Recipe Finder

The application that will make cooking a breeze. With Recipe Finder you can easily find recipes that you can cook based on your available products.
You can also add products into the fridge and have searches based on them or to remind yourself what to buy.

Features

  • Search among thousand of recipes based on what products you have.
  • List what products you have available at home.
  • Take a picture of the finished product and share it.

Getting started

Using this application is exremely easy. To search for recipes click the "Find me a recipe button".
To see what products you have in your fridge just click the refrigerator button.
To make a photo of the product click the camera button.
